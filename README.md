## Traditional banking is reactive. SBI FinTwin is proactive.

Every financial journey begins with a dream.

Buying a home.

Building a business.

Funding education.

Planning retirement.

Securing a family's future.

But between every dream and reality lies uncertainty.

SBI FinTwin is built to eliminate that uncertainty.

Powered by **Astra**, SBI FinTwin creates a living Financial Twin for every customer—continuously learning from their financial behavior, adapting to changing market conditions, and proactively guiding them toward smarter financial decisions.

Rather than simply helping customers perform transactions, SBI FinTwin empowers them to build a better financial future with confidence.

# 💡 What is Astra?

Astra is the intelligence behind SBI FinTwin.

It isn't just another AI chatbot.

It isn't just another financial assistant.

Astra is your Financial Twin.

A living digital representation of your financial life that continuously understands:

- Income
- Expenses
- Savings
- Investments
- Financial Goals
- Loans & EMIs
- Spending Patterns
- Risk Profile
- Life Events

Along with continuously monitoring:

- Loan Interest Rates
- FD Interest Rates
- Inflation
- Government Schemes
- Market Trends
- Investment Opportunities

Astra proactively provides personalized financial recommendations that evolve as your life changes.
